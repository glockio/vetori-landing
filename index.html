Frequently asked questions

What data do you support? ▾
Currently CSV. Upload impact, program, funding, or location data and Vetori auto-detects columns, field types, and coordinates.
Can we embed Vetori on our current website or CMS? ▾
Yes. You get an embed code for any website or CMS, plus a self-hosted HTML export with zero dependencies if your web team wants full control.
Can donors and visitors interact with the visual? ▾
Yes. Viewers can filter, search, click data points, zoom, and pan. Everything runs in their browser.
Can we match our organization's brand? ▾
Yes. Customize colors and presentation to match your site. Paid plans remove Vetori branding completely.
Do we need a developer to launch this? ▾
No. Vetori is designed for teams that need a public-facing impact visual without commissioning a custom interactive build.
Can we start with one chart now and add guided stories later? ▾
Yes. Vetori is built so you can launch a single embeddable map or chart now. Guided narrative publishing is the next step on the roadmap.
