Frequently asked questions

Who is Vetori for? ▾
Vetori is best for operations, finance, agency, consulting, and program teams that send recurring internal, executive, client, or partner reports.
What data do you support? ▾
Currently CSV. Upload impact, program, funding, or location data and Vetori auto-detects columns, field types, and coordinates.
Can we share reports privately or publicly? ▾
Yes. Vetori is built for secure recipient delivery first, while still supporting web embeds and a self-hosted HTML export for controlled handoff.
Do viewers need a full Vetori account? ▾
No. Vetori is designed so recipients can access shared reports through a dedicated viewer flow instead of needing authoring access.
Can viewers interact with the report? ▾
Yes. Viewers can filter, search, click data points, zoom, and pan. Everything runs in their browser.
Can we match the brand for each report? ▾
Yes. Customize colors and presentation to match the client site, destination site, campaign, or reporting context. Paid plans remove Vetori branding completely.
Does the export work outside Vetori? ▾
Yes. Vetori exports a self-hosted HTML artifact designed for portability, so teams can host or hand off the report outside their Vetori account.
Do we need a developer for every report? ▾
No. Vetori is designed for teams that need polished recurring reports without commissioning a custom interactive build every time.
Are stories live yet? ▾
Today the focus is charts and dashboards you can deliver as private or public report artifacts. Guided stories and narrative playback are the next step on the roadmap.
