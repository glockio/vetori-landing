Reporting is the recurring fire drill nobody fixed.

Finance teams spend ~85% of their cycle on assembly and ~15% on insight. Deal teams pull all-nighters to the v44. Consulting analysts spend Friday on a deck instead of analysis. Same shape of pain, three industries.

Associate wants one thing, VP wants another, MD wants something else. Three to four weeks of revisions. Most of it doesn't change the deal — but the deck still has to ship.

08 — FAQ

Questions buyers actually ask

Who is Vetori for? ▾
Finance leaders (CFO, VP Finance, FP&A), deal teams (M&A advisory, corp dev, IB), and consulting partners — anyone whose monthly or quarterly deliverable is a report that lands in front of an executive.
Can the audience interact with the report? ▾
Yes. Recipients can play the narrated story OR take over and explore — filter, search, drill into a row, change a dimension. Same artifact, two consumption modes.
How is this different from Tableau or Looker? ▾
Tableau and Looker are BI tools for analysts. Vetori is a delivery layer for executives. We don't replace your data warehouse — we replace the slide deck your analyst makes from your data warehouse.
How is this different from Flourish or Gamma? ▾
Flourish makes pretty charts; Gamma makes pretty slides. Neither connects to your live data, neither manages per-recipient access, and neither generates the report from your CSV. Vetori does all three.
What data formats do you support? ▾
Currently CSV. Vetori auto-detects columns, types, and coordinates. Native data-warehouse connectors are on the roadmap.
Are AI-authored stories live yet? ▾
Yes. AI-authored stories ship in the closed beta. Audio narration over the playback is the next item on the roadmap.
What if the AI hallucinates the data? ▾
The AI works from your CSV — no hallucination on numbers. You review and approve every narrative before it ships. Audit trail logs all versions.
Do recipients need an account? ▾
No. Recipients access shared reports through a dedicated viewer flow — magic-link or password-protected — without needing an authoring account.
Can we match the brand for each report? ▾
Yes. Custom subdomain, tunable colors, paid plans remove Vetori branding entirely. Each report can be branded for the specific client or stakeholder.
