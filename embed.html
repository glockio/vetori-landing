WHERE EMBEDS FIT

A better public publishing path than screenshots or custom one-offs.

The embed workflow makes the most sense when distribution matters more than access control. These are the teams most likely to use it first.

MARKETING TEAMS

Launch pages that need a live chart, not a static image.

Publish product metrics, benchmark snapshots, impact counters, or campaign results inside the page the audience is already reading.

Best fit when speed beats bespoke frontend work.
AGENCIES AND STUDIOS

Ship client-facing visuals without building chart components from scratch.

Use Vetori for recurring embeds across campaigns, content hubs, proposals, and public microsites where handoff and iteration both matter.

Strong fit for teams willing to pay to reduce production overhead.
NONPROFITS AND PROGRAM TEAMS

Turn spreadsheet-backed updates into donor and public-facing reporting.

Share progress publicly through campaign pages, annual updates, or resource portals while keeping the authoring workflow lightweight.

Useful when the public web is the destination, not the byproduct.
PUBLIC VS PRIVATE

Two distribution modes, one product family.

Vetori Embeds is the lightweight public motion. The main Vetori product stays focused on secure private delivery for internal, executive, and client reports.

VETORI EMBEDS

Public by default

self-serve

Best for websites, campaigns, news posts, public updates, and content marketing.

Use when viewers do not need authentication and the main goal is reach, clarity, and faster publishing.

Outputs include an embed snippet, a public share page, and a portable artifact for lightweight handoff.

SECURE REPORTS

Private by design

core product

Best for internal reporting, executive updates, client dashboards, board materials, and sensitive external delivery.

Use when access control, recipient experience, and recurring stakeholder distribution matter more than open-web reach.

This is the primary Vetori brand and the natural upgrade path once a report should not live on the public internet.

WHAT YOU GET

Lightweight distribution, not a throwaway export.

The public workflow still benefits from the same publication system: theming, layout polish, portable output, and a predictable path from data to final asset.

RESPONSIVE EMBEDS

Iframe output that is easy to ship.

SHARE PAGE FALLBACK

PORTABLE HTML

The friction stays low for teams that want the result without buying into a heavyweight BI workflow.

HOW IT WORKS

From spreadsheet to public embed in three steps.

The workflow is intentionally narrow: get structured data in, shape a chart worth publishing, and export the right public-facing output.

1

Upload CSV data

Start with the same source teams already use for public updates, campaign reporting, benchmarks, or impact snapshots.

2

Shape the chart and theme

Turn raw data into a presentation-ready visual without having to build or maintain the visualization layer yourself.

3

Publish and distribute

Copy the embed code, share the public page, or hand off an HTML artifact depending on how the destination site works.
